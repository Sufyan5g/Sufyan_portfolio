Case Study: Corporate Website for Innovate Architects

Client: Innovate Architects
Service: Web Design & Development
Result: 95+ Google PageSpeed Score

The Challenge: An Outdated Digital Blueprint

Innovate Architects aik top-tier architecture firm thi, lekin unki website purani, slow, aur mobile par theek se nahi chalti thi. Unki digital presence unke real-world projects ki quality ko reflect nahi kar rahi thi.

My Approach: Engineering a Digital Masterpiece

Mera goal aik aisi website banana tha jo design mein minimalist aur performance mein maximalist ho. Maine website ko modern HTML5, CSS3, aur Vanilla JavaScript ka istemal karke 'from scratch' banaya. Sabhi images ko WebP format mein convert kiya gaya aur "lazy loading" implement ki gayi taake site lightning-fast rahe.

The Results: A Foundation for Growth

Nayi website launch hote hi aik game-changer saabit hui. Website ne Google PageSpeed Insights par 95+ ka score hasil kiya, bounce rate mein 40% kami aayi, aur client inquiries mein 50% ka izafa hua. Nayi website ab unke liye aik powerful client-acquisition tool ban chuki hai.

Case Study: E-commerce SEO Overhaul

Client: Artisan Crafts PK
Service: Strategic SEO
Result: 300% Increase in Organic Traffic
Achievement: Ranked #1 for 15+ High-Value Keywords

The Challenge: An Invisible Gem

Artisan Crafts PK ke paas behtareen, handmade products theen, lekin unki online presence na hone ke barabar thi. Unki website ahem keywords ke liye rank nahi kar rahi thi aur woh bari e-commerce websites ke shor mein gum ho gaye the. Unhe aisi strategy chahiye thi jo unhe unke target audience tak pohnchaye.

My Strategic Solution: A 3-Phase SEO Attack

Mera plan sirf keywords daalna nahi tha, balke aik mukammal 'authority' build karna tha.

  1. Phase 1: Technical & Keyword Audit: Maine website ka gehra technical SEO audit kiya, site speed, mobile-friendliness, aur crawlability issues ko theek kiya. Iske saath, maine competitor analysis aur keyword research karke un "golden keywords" ko target kiya jin par rank karna mumkin tha.
  2. Phase 2: On-Page Dominance: Har product page, category page, aur blog post ko SEO ke liye optimize kiya gaya. Ismein meta titles, descriptions, image alt tags, aur internal linking structure shamil tha.
  3. Phase 3: Authority Building: Maine high-quality, relevant websites se backlinks hasil karne ke liye aik content marketing aur outreach campaign chalaya. Is se Google ki nazron mein unki website ki value barh gayi.

The Results: From Invisible to Invincible

Mehnat rang laayi. 6 maheenon ke andar, client ne zabardast نتایج dekhe:

  • Organic traffic mein 300% ka izafa hua.
  • Website "handmade leather bags in Pakistan" jaise 15 se zyada ahem keywords ke liye Google ke pehle page par rank karne lagi.
  • Organic sales mein 2x (100%) ka izafa hua, kyunke ab unke paas aane wali traffic high-quality thi.

Artisan Crafts PK ab sirf aik aur online store nahi, balke apni niche mein aik pehchana jaane wala brand ban chuka tha.

Case Study: E-commerce SEO Overhaul

Client: Artisan Crafts PK
Service: Strategic SEO
Result: 300% Increase in Organic Traffic

The Challenge: An Invisible Gem

Artisan Crafts PK ke paas behtareen, handmade products theen, lekin unki online presence na hone ke barabar thi. Unki website ahem keywords ke liye rank nahi kar rahi thi aur woh bari e-commerce websites ke shor mein gum ho gaye the.

My Strategic Solution: A 3-Phase SEO Attack

Mera plan sirf keywords daalna nahi tha, balke aik mukammal 'authority' build karna tha. Maine website ka gehra technical SEO audit kiya, site speed issues ko theek kiya, on-page content ko optimize kiya, aur high-quality backlinks hasil karne ke liye aik content marketing campaign chalaya.

The Results: From Invisible to Invincible

Mehnat rang laayi. 6 maheenon ke andar, client ne zabardast نتایج dekhe: Organic traffic mein 300% ka izafa hua, website 15 se zyada ahem keywords ke liye Google ke pehle page par rank karne lagi, aur organic sales mein 100% ka izafa hua.

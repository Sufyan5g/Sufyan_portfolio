Case Study: AI Customer Service Bot

Client: TechSolutions Inc.
Service: AI Development
Result: 40% Reduction in Support Tickets

The Challenge

Client ko 24/7 customer support provide karne mein mushkil ho rahi thi aur unke support agents par bohat load tha. Unhe aik aisa solution chahiye tha jo aam sawalat ko foran handle kar sake.

The Solution

Maine Python aur Google's Dialogflow ka istemal karke aik intelligent chatbot banaya. Yeh chatbot client ki website mein integrate kiya gaya jo users ke sawalat ko samajh kar foran jawab deta tha. Complex queries ke liye, yeh bot conversation ko live agent ko transfer kar deta tha.

The Results

Launch ke 3 maheenay ke andar, is AI bot ne customer queries mein 40% tak kami ki. Is se na sirf company ke kharche kam hue, balke customer satisfaction bhi behtar hui kyunke unhe foran jawab mil rahe the.
